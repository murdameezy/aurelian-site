Mission

To narrow the wealth gap by putting institutional-grade quant strategies in the hands of small-account traders—without them surrendering custody of their funds.

How the bot works (30 sec)

  1. You connect a paper Alpaca account (live trading coming soon).
  2. Our cloud engine screens 7 000 symbols each morning for liquidity + volatility, then applies our momentum filter.
  3. Position size is capped at 2 % per ticker to manage risk.
  4. Trades execute in your account; you can pause or disconnect anytime.
